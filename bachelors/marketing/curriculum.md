## ᚱ Core Theories & Frameworks

### Classic Marketing Theory (Still Relevant)
- **4P → 7P → 4C → 4E** — Product/Price/Place/Promotion evolved for experience economy
- **Segmentation, Targeting, Positioning (STP)** — still the strategic backbone
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)** — the north star metric
- **Marketing funnel → Flywheel** — from linear acquisition to circular retention
- **Brand equity** — Aaker's model: awareness, associations, perceived quality, loyalty

### Behavioral Economics in Marketing
- **Nudge theory** — choice architecture that guides without restricting (Thaler)
- **Anchoring** — first price presented anchors perception
- **Loss aversion** — fear of losing > desire of gaining (Kahneman & Tversky)
- **Social proof** — reviews, testimonials, "X people bought this"
- **Scarcity & urgency** — limited time, limited quantity triggers action
- **Endowment effect** — ownership increases perceived value

### 2040 Marketing Paradigms
- **Hyper-personalization** — AI generates unique content per individual based on preference model
- **Neural preference mapping** — fMRI/EEG-derived models of what people actually respond to
- **Agent-to-agent marketing** — AI agents negotiate product recommendations on behalf of users
- **Reputation-based discovery** — products surface via community trust, not ad spend
- **Experience-as-product** — the marketing IS the product (e.g., unboxing as content)

## ᛏ 2040-Era Advances

### AI-Native Marketing
- **Generative AI campaigns** — infinite variations of ad creative, A/B tested at scale
- **Predictive churn modeling** — know who will leave before they do
- **Conversational commerce** — AI agents handle sales conversations end-to-end
- **Sentiment-aware pricing** — dynamic pricing based on real-time social mood
- **Synthetic influencers** — AI-generated brand ambassadors with consistent personas

### Privacy-First Marketing
- **Zero-party data** — customers voluntarily share preferences in exchange for value
- **Post-cookie targeting** — contextual, cohort-based, and consent-driven targeting
- **Federated learning** — model training on-device, data stays local
- **Transparent AI** — explainable recommendation systems, no black-box persuasion

### The Reputation Economy
- **Contribution scoring** — DAO-style reputation tracks real contributions, not follower counts
- **Community-owned brands** — token-gated communities that co-create products
- **Anti-hype culture** — authenticity premium; over-promising backfires faster than ever
- **Micro-influence** — 100 true fans > 1M passive followers
