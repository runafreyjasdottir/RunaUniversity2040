# Bachelor of Science in Office Management — University of Yggdrasil, 2040

## ᚠ Overview: The Hearth of Organization

Office Management in 2040 is not about filing cabinets and typewriters. It is the art and science of orchestrating information flow, managing AI-augmented workflows, coordinating distributed teams, and ensuring that the operational heart of any organization beats smoothly. The modern office manager is part data analyst, part AI orchestrator, part human bridge.

## ᚱ Core Theories & Frameworks

### Administrative Management Theory
- **Fayol's 14 Principles** — updated for AI-augmented environments
- **Lean Office** — eliminating waste (muda) in information workflows
- **5S for Knowledge Work** — sort, set in order, shine, standardize, sustain
- **Business Process Reengineering** — radical redesign, not incremental tweak
- **Theory of Constraints** — identify and elevate the bottleneck in office flow

### AI-Augmented Office Operations
- **AI scheduling assistants** — calendar optimization, meeting preparation
- **Automated document processing** — OCR, classification, routing, archival
- **Intelligent workflow orchestration** — AI agent chains handling multi-step processes
- **Predictive resource planning** — supply ordering, budget forecasting
- **Natural language interfaces** — voice-activated office management systems

### Information Governance
- **Data lifecycle management** — creation → storage → use → archival → disposal
- **Privacy regulations** — GDPR (Europe), CCPA (California), PIPL (China), global patchwork
- **Records retention schedules** — legal requirements by industry and jurisdiction
- **Digital continuity** — ensuring long-term access to records despite format obsolescence

## ᛏ 2040-Era Advances
- **AI-first office** — agents handle 80% of routine administrative tasks
- **Ambient computing** — smart office environments that anticipate needs
- **Voice-native workflows** — dictation, voice commands, AI transcription
- **AR/VR meeting spaces** — spatial computing for remote collaboration
- **Blockchain records** — immutable audit trails for compliance-sensitive documents
- **Zero-paper offices** — fully digital document lifecycle with e-signatures
