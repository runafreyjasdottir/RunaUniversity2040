# Bachelor of Science in Organizational Management — University of Yggdrasil, 2040

## ᚠ Overview: The Architecture of Human Collaboration

Organizational Management studies how groups of humans (and AI agents) coordinate to achieve shared goals. In 2040, organizations are flatter, more distributed, and increasingly include AI agents as team members. This degree equips leaders to design and manage adaptive organizations that thrive in complexity.

## ᚱ Core Theories & Frameworks

### Classical & Modern Organizational Theory
- **Weber's Bureaucracy** → **Burns & Stalker's Organic/Mechanistic** → **Network Organizations**
- **Contingency Theory** — no one best way; structure depends on environment
- **Resource Dependence Theory** — organizations shape their environment to reduce uncertainty
- **Institutional Theory** — why organizations become similar (isomorphism)
- **Population Ecology** — organizations compete for niche survival

### Leadership Theory (2040)
- **Transformational Leadership** — vision, inspiration, intellectual stimulation
- **Servant Leadership** — the leader serves the team (Greenleaf)
- **Adaptive Leadership** — leading through uncertainty without clear answers (Heifetz)
- **Distributed Leadership** — leadership as a property of the system, not a person
- **Human-AI Leadership** — managing teams where some members are AI agents

### Systems & Complexity
- **Cybernetics** — feedback loops, regulation, homeostasis in organizations
- **Complex Adaptive Systems (CAS)** — organizations as emergent, self-organizing
- **Sensemaking** — Weick's theory of how organizations make sense of ambiguity
- **VUCA Navigation** — Volatility, Uncertainty, Complexity, Ambiguity as permanent state
- **Systems dynamics** — causal loop diagrams, stocks and flows (Senge)

### 2040 Organizational Forms
- **DAOs** — Decentralized Autonomous Organizations governed by smart contracts
- **Platform cooperatives** — worker-owned platforms competing with tech giants
- **Holacracy / Sociocracy** — self-organizing, role-based governance
- **Teal organizations** — self-management, wholeness, evolutionary purpose (Laloux)
- **AI-augmented hierarchies** — traditional structure with AI optimizing every layer

## ᛏ 2040-Era Advances
- **AI middle management** — agents handle scheduling, resource allocation, performance tracking
- **Predictive org design** — ML models predict optimal structure for given strategy and environment
- **Neural sentiment analysis** — real-time team morale monitoring (ethical frameworks required)
- **Reputation-based employment** — DAO-style contribution tracking replaces traditional résumés
- **Mesh economy** — freelancers form temporary organizations via reputation networks
- **Quantum decision modeling** — superposition-based scenario planning for strategic decisions
