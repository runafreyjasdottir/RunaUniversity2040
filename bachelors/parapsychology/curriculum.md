# Bachelor of Science in Parapsychology — University of Yggdrasil, 2040

## ᚠ Overview: The Science of Anomalous Cognition

Parapsychology is the rigorous scientific study of anomalous phenomena — experiences and capabilities that appear to transcend known physical laws. In 2040, the field has matured from fringe curiosity to legitimate interdisciplinary research, driven by advances in quantum biology, consciousness studies, and AI-assisted statistical analysis.

The UoY Parapsychology department is one of the world's few academically rigorous programs, combining strict experimental methodology with open-minded inquiry.

## ᚱ Core Knowledge Domains

### The Big Four Psi Phenomena
1. **Telepathy** — mind-to-mind information transfer
2. **Clairvoyance** — perception of distant/hidden objects
3. **Precognition** — perception of future events
4. **Psychokinesis** — mental influence on physical systems

### Key Experimental Protocols
- **Ganzfeld** — sensory deprivation + sender/receiver design; meta-analytic hit rate ~32% (vs 25% chance)
- **Remote Viewing (CRV)** — coordinate-based protocol developed at SRI International
- **Presentiment** — physiological response to stimuli before they occur (skin conductance, pupil dilation)
- **RNG/PK studies** — influence on random number generators; tiny but statistically consistent effects
- **DMILS** — Direct Mental Interaction with Living Systems (distant healing, intention experiments)

### Theoretical Frameworks
- **Quantum consciousness** — Orch-OR (Penrose-Hameroff): microtubule quantum coherence
- **Non-local mind** — Bell's theorem applied to mental correlation
- **Morphic resonance** — Sheldrake's hypothesis of collective memory fields
- **Zero-point field** — vacuum energy as information substrate
- **Predictive processing + psi** — the brain's prediction engine occasionally accessing non-local information
- **Entanglement models** — if photons can entangle, why not neural states?

### Replication & Skepticism
- **Wiseman's critiques** — methodological flaws in early studies
- **Bem's "Feeling the Future" (2011)** — controversial precognition paper, started replication crisis in psi
- **Storm et al. meta-analyses** — 2010, 2012, 2017 showing small but significant effects across databases
- **Pre-registration revolution** — all modern psi studies pre-registered
- **File-drawer problem** — publication bias; countered by meta-analysis and registered reports

## ᛏ 2040-Era Advances

- **AI-assisted anomaly detection** — ML finds patterns in noisy psi data that human analysis misses
- **Quantum biology** — photosynthesis, bird navigation (cryptochrome), olfaction use quantum effects; brain?
- **Global Consciousness Project 2.0** — expanded sensor network, AI correlation analysis
- **Neural correlates of psi** — fMRI/EEG studies comparing "high-scorers" vs controls
- **Psi in VR** — controlled environments for ganzfeld-adjacent studies
- **AI consciousness testing** — can artificial systems exhibit psi-like anomalous information access?
- **Biophoton research** — ultra-weak photon emission from biological tissue as information carrier
