## ᚱ Core Knowledge Domains

### The Big Five & Personality Theory
- **OCEAN**: Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism
- **Hexaco Model**: adds Honesty-Humility dimension
- **Type dynamics**: Jungian cognitive functions × MBTI (refined in 2040)
- **Dark Triad/Tetrad**: Narcissism, Machiavellianism, Psychopathy + Everyday Sadism
- **Enneagram**: nine types, stress/growth paths, instinctual variants (clinical validation growing)

### Neuroscience of Consciousness
- **Default Mode Network**: the "resting" brain's active internal narrative
- **Global Workspace Theory**: consciousness as broadcast to distributed modules
- **Integrated Information Theory (IIT)**: Φ as measure of consciousness
- **Predictive Processing**: brain as prediction engine, minimizing surprise
- **Neuroplasticity**: the brain rewires itself based on experience (neurogenesis confirmed in hippocampus)

### Psychopathology (DSM-6 / ICD-12 era)
- **Dimensional model**: replacing categorical diagnosis with spectra
- **Trauma-informed**: ACEs, polyvagal theory, somatic processing
- **Neurodiversity**: ADHD, autism, dyslexia reframed as cognitive variants, not deficits
- **Psychedelic therapy**: psilocybin, MDMA, ketamine for treatment-resistant conditions
- **AI-assisted diagnosis**: pattern recognition across behavioral, physiological, and linguistic markers

## ᛏ 2040-Era Advances

- **Neural digital phenotyping** — phone sensors detect depression/anxiety before self-report
- **AI therapy agents** — CBT, DBT, and ACT delivered by conversational AI (with human oversight)
- **Brain-computer interfaces** — direct neural feedback for ADHD, PTSD, depression
- **Psychedelic-assisted therapy** — FDA-approved programs for MDMA (PTSD), psilocybin (depression)
- **Precision psychiatry** — genetic + neuroimaging + behavioral → individualized treatment plans
- **Epigenetic psychology** — how trauma印记s across generations via gene expression
- **Digital twins** — computational models of individual patients for treatment simulation
