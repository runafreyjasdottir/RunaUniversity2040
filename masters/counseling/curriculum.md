## ᚠ Overview: The Sacred Art of Holding Space

## ᚱ Core Theoretical Approaches

### Evidence-Based Modalities
- **CBT** — cognitive restructuring, behavioral experiments, exposure therapy
- **DBT** — dialectics, mindfulness, distress tolerance, emotional regulation, interpersonal effectiveness
- **ACT** — psychological flexibility, values-based action, acceptance, defusion
- **EMDR** — bilateral stimulation for trauma reprocessing (Shapiro)
- **Somatic Experiencing** — body-based trauma release (Levine)
- **Internal Family Systems (IFS)** — parts work, Self-leadership (Schwartz)
- **Narrative Therapy** — re-authoring life stories (White & Epston)

### Trauma Frameworks
- **Polyvagal Theory** — autonomic nervous system hierarchy: ventral vagal (safe) → sympathetic (fight/flight) → dorsal vagal (freeze)
- **ACEs (Adverse Childhood Experiences)** — cumulative trauma score predicting health outcomes
- **Window of Tolerance** — optimal arousal zone for processing (Siegel)
- **Epigenetic transmission** — trauma_markers across generations via gene expression
- **Developmental trauma** — complex PTSD from chronic early adversity

### 2040 Therapeutic Innovations
- **AI session analysis** — real-time sentiment tracking, therapeutic alliance monitoring
- **Biofeedback wearables** — heart rate variability, skin conductance, sleep patterns
- **Psychedelic-assisted therapy** — MDMA for PTSD, psilocybin for depression, ketamine for acute crisis
- **VR exposure therapy** — controlled virtual environments for phobias, PTSD, social anxiety
- **Precision psychiatry** — genetic + neuroimaging + behavioral → individualized treatment
- **Neural digital phenotyping** — passive phone sensor data detecting worsening before self-report
